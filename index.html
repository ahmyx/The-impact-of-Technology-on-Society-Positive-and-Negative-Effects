The Impact of Technology on Society

Home About

Technology has revolutionized the way we live, work, and interact. While it brings many advantages, it also has some drawbacks.

Positive Effects

  • Improved communication through social media and smartphones
  • Advancements in medical technology save lives
  • Increased access to education and information
  • Automation improves efficiency in industries

Negative Effects

  • Overdependence on digital devices
  • Privacy concerns and cybercrime
  • Job losses due to automation
  • Social isolation and mental health issues
